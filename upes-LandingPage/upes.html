UPES

The University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) in Dehradun is a leading institution offering specialized courses in energy, petroleum, and allied sectors. Renowned for its innovative programs, UPES focuses on practical learning, industry exposure, and research. With state-of-the-art facilities and a commitment to fostering industry-ready professionals, UPES stands as a distinguished choice for those seeking education in the dynamic fields of energy and petroleum.Established in 2003, UPES Dehradun has emerged as a premier university in India, providing courses in areas such as engineering, business, law, and design. The institution's unique approach integrates industry collaboration and real-world experiences, preparing students for the demands of their respective fields. With a picturesque campus nestled in the foothills of the Himalayas, UPES combines academic excellence with a vibrant learning environment. It consistently ranks among the top universities for energy and core sectors education, attracting students seeking a dynamic and career-focused education in a variety of disciplines.

Courses Offered by the university:

  • B.Tech in Petroleum Engineering
  • B.Tech in Energy Engineering
  • B.Tech in Computer Science Engineering
  • B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
  • B.Tech in Civil Engineering
  • BBA in Oil and Gas Marketing
  • BBA in Aviation Operations
  • LLB in Energy Laws
  • LLM in International Business Law
